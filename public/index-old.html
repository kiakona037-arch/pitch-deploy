SONAR CONVENTIONS // THE PROBLEM

The Problem

Convention centers monetize square footage — but not digital attention inside the building.

💰 High Exhibitor Costs
Exhibitors invest $30K-$100K per event in booth space, travel, and sponsorships — with significant financial risk.
👁 Zero Attendee Insight
Exhibitors have no visibility into who walks past their booth, what they're looking for, or whether they're a qualified lead.
📉 Uncertain ROI
High costs + zero data = impossible to justify event ROI to stakeholders. Every event is a gamble.

SONAR CONVENTIONS // THE SOLUTION

The Solution

Turn every booth into a measurable engagement hub — with zero hardware.
