SONAR CONVENTIONS // THE PROBLEM

Two Problems

At conventions, vendors can't find the right attendees and attendees can't find the right vendors — so everyone wastes time, effort, and money.

💰 High Costs
● Exhibitors invest $30K-$100K per event in booth space, travel, and sponsorships — with significant financial risk.
● Attendees spend thousands on travel, time, and conference fees — with no guarantee of meaningful connections.
👁 Zero Visibility
● Exhibitors have no visibility into who walks past their booth, what they're looking for, or whether they're a qualified lead.
● Attendees have no way to discover which booths match their interests — leading to aimless wandering and missed opportunities.
📉 Uncertain ROI
● Exhibitors can't justify event spend to stakeholders — high costs + zero data makes every event a gamble.
● Attendees leave unsure if the trip was worth it — no way to measure whether they made the right connections.
